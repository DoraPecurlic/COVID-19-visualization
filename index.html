ABOUT COVID-19

The goal of this project is to illustrate the spread and impact of the COVID-19 virus across the United States.

By clicking on "Infection" you can view a map of the U.S. that shows the COVID-19 infection rate by state during the initial months of the outbreak (from January 1, 2020, to May 29, 2020). Additionally, by clicking on "Mortality" a similar visualization is displayed, showing the mortality rate in the U.S. over the same period.

A comparison with other diseases is provided through a bar chart, which can be accessed via the "Comparison" link.
